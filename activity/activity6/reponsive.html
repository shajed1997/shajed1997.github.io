baby's breath flowers

Gypsophila paniculata, the baby's breath, common gypsophila or panicled baby's-breath, is a species of flowering plant in the family Caryophyllaceae, native to central and eastern Europe.It is an herbaceous perennial growing to 1.2 m (4 ft) tall and wide,with mounds of branching stems covered in clouds of tiny white flowers in summer (hence the common name "baby's breath")

blue bird

The Eastern Bluebird is a brightly colored blue and red bird. The male bird's head, wings, and tail are blue, but its belly is red and white. The legs and feet are black. Also, its beak is yellow and black. The female's head is gray or brown and its colors are paler than the male's and younger birds are brown with some white and blue plumage.
